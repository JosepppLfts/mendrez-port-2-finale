SwiftDrop is a local delivery service proudly based in Magdugo, Toledo City. We started with a simple mission: make deliveries faster, cheaper, and more reliable for our community.

Whether you're sending food, groceries, documents, or gifts — our riders are on standby to get it there fast. We serve Magdugo and all surrounding barangays in Toledo City.
