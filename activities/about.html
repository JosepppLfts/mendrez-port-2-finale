About Us

The story behind Joseph's Saging Shop

Our Story

Joseph's Saging Shop started as a small roadside stall with one simple goal: bring the freshest bananas from local farms directly to our community. Founded near CIT-U in Labangon, Cebu City, what began as a passion project has grown into a trusted neighborhood source for quality produce.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to fresh, affordable, and nutritious food. By partnering with local Filipino farmers, we support sustainable agriculture while giving our customers the best bananas at fair prices.

Our Values

Freshness, honesty, and community are at the heart of everything we do. We hand-pick every bunch, maintain close relationships with our farming partners, and treat every customer like family.
